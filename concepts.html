Core Web3 Dynamics

The philosophical and technical building blocks defining decentralization.

Web2 vs Web3

Who actually owns your data?

Architectural Shift

Web2

Companies permanently own your core data frames.

Accounts can be suspended arbitrarily.

Strictly requires trust in centralized governance.

Single points of failure risk.

Web3

You inherently own your data via cryptography.

Fundamentally censorship-resistant networks.

Trustless. Math enforces execution locally.

Decentralized globally distributed node operations.

💡 Key Insight: In Web3, your wallet IS your identity.

Ethereum vs Bitcoin

The Gold versus The Computer.

Network Typology

Bitcoin (₿)

Digital Gold. Premier global store of value.

Strictly ~7 TPS. Proof of Work (PoW).

Very limited primitive scripting support.

Capped emission: strictly 21M coins.

Ethereum (♦)

Programmable Money. World supercomputer.

High throughput via rollups. Proof of Stake (PoS).

Turing-complete smart contract deployments.

Powers the entirety of modern DeFi & DAOs.

💡 Key Insight: Bitcoin is a currency. Ethereum is an operating system.

Public vs Private Keys

How cryptographic possession physically works.

Cryptography

Public Key

Serves as your raw blockchain identifier.

100% safe to disseminate publicly.

Acts functionally like your bank account routing number.

Private Key

Massive 256-bit arbitrary mathematical coordinate.

Mathematically signs every outgoing transaction.

🚨 WARNING: Never share your private key. Not even with support. If exposed, funds drain instantly.

Chain vs Database

Why not just use Postgres?

Data Storage Typology

Traditional Database

Dictated violently by a singular central sysadmin.

Can freely delete, redact, or rewrite history.

Extremely fast high-frequency reads/writes.

Relies heavily on trusted third-party security.

Blockchain Ledger

No administrator physically exists.

Immutable append-only chronological sequences.

Slower throughput but objectively verifiable.

Trustless execution enforced via consensus algorithms.

💡 Key Insight: A blockchain trades speed and scale for absolute trust framework elimination.
